Fig. 03 · The Breach · 16-team diamond, animated

All cells start dark. That is the color of exclusion.

Press play. Cells light in the order each franchise signed its first Black player. Brooklyn first. Cleveland a season later. Then a pause. Then a wave. Then the long, dark tail held by the Yankees, the Phillies, the Tigers, and -- for twelve years, three months, six days -- the Boston Red Sox.
