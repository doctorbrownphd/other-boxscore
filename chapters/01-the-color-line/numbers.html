Fig. 04 · Five categories, drawn as lanes

The names on the left held the records. The names on the right always earned them.

Each lane is a category. Each dot is a player, placed at the position his number puts him on the axis. Pre-2024, the lanes carry only Major League players. Flip the switch, and the dots appear that the bookkeepers had not been counting. None of them moves. They were already there.
