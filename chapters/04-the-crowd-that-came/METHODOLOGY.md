## 1. Attendance Data

### Primary Source

Lester, Larry. *Black Baseball's National Showcase: The East-West
All-Star Game, 1933--1962.* Expanded edition. NoirTech Research, 2020.

All East-West attendance figures in this chapter are verified against
Lester's primary text. No secondary-source figure ships without
cross-reference to the primary.

### Secondary Sources

- Retrosheet Negro League All-Star Games (public domain)
- Baseball Almanac MLB All-Star Game attendance (public domain)
- SABR game accounts and journal articles (open access)

### Cross-Reference Protocol

Every year-by-year figure is checked against at least two sources.
Discrepancies are documented in the table below.

| Year | Lester | Retrosheet | Baseball Almanac | Discrepancy | Resolution |
|------|--------|------------|------------------|-------------|------------|
| 1933 | -- | -- | -- | -- | Pending Oscar review |
| ... | -- | -- | -- | -- | -- |
| 1948 | -- | -- | -- | -- | -- |

### Known Complications

- **1945:** No MLB All-Star Game held due to wartime travel restrictions.
  The East-West Game was played. Both facts are documented and noted
  in the chart.
- **1941 vs 1943 peak confusion:** Secondary sources occasionally
  conflate the two highest attendance years. 50,256 (1941) and 51,723
  (1943) are separate years. Both verified against Lester.

## 2. Roster Data

### 1943 East-West All-Star Game Roster

Player names, team affiliations, and positions are verified against
Lester (2020) and cross-referenced with Retrosheet game accounts.

### Hall of Fame Status

HOF induction years verified against the National Baseball Hall of
Fame and Museum's official records. Each inducted player's year
confirmed individually.

### HOF Gap Calculation

Players marked with the documented-gap indicator meet all of the
following criteria:

1. Appeared in East-West All-Star Game(s)
2. Career statistics documented in Seamheads or comparable databases
3. Statistics meet or exceed the threshold of the lowest-credentialed
   inducted Negro Leagues player at the same position
4. Player was never inducted into the Hall of Fame

**This is model output, not fact.** The calculation uses the same
methodology as the Criteria Compliance Model from the One Long
Impersonation platform, adapted for baseball. Oscar reviews the
criteria application. Elias reviews the calculation.

## 3. Press Coverage Documentation

### Black Press Sources

Coverage claims are sourced to specific dated editions:

- Chicago Defender -- ProQuest Historical Newspapers
- Pittsburgh Courier -- ProQuest Historical Newspapers
- Amsterdam News -- ProQuest Historical Newspapers

Every headline, column count, and placement claim traces to a
specific dated issue.

### Mainstream Press Absence

Documented absences are cited with:

- Named publication(s)
- Specific date range checked
- Archive searched (e.g., ProQuest, newspaper.com)

An absence of coverage, documented with the specific search
parameters, is itself evidence.
